wumbo.tech has a new home. The site has been re-platformed onto
[Astro](https://astro.build/) with the [AstroPaper](https://github.com/satnaing/astro-paper)
theme, and its words are now managed through [Pages CMS](https://pagescms.org/).

## What changed

- **Faster, simpler, static.** Every page is pre-rendered, so the site is quick
  and cheap to serve.
- **A real blog.** There's more to wumbology than a single definition, and now
  there's somewhere to put it.
- **Git-backed editing.** Posts live as Markdown in the repository. Edit them in
  Pages CMS, and changes are committed straight to GitHub.

## What stayed the same

The mission. wumbo.tech remains the worldwide leader in wumbology — the study of
wumbo. Expect field notes, lore, and the occasional magnifying glass.

I wumbo, you wumbo, he/she/me wumbo.
