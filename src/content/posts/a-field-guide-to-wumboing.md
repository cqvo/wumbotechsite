Wumboing is the act of wumbo, in progress. It is the practical, hands-on branch
of wumbology, and — unlike the theory — anyone can start today.

## Table of contents

## The first rule of wumbo

You already know it: _I wumbo, you wumbo, he/she/me wumbo._ Conjugation is the
beginning of confidence, and confidence is most of wumboing.

## A simple practice

1. Find something small.
2. Point at it.
3. Declare, out loud, your intent to make it wumbo.
4. Embiggen accordingly.

When done correctly, the small thing is now wumbo. When done _spectacularly_,
you have achieved Wumborama.

## Common mistakes

- Underestimating the magnifying glass.
- Forgetting that wumbo is, at its heart, a state of mind.
- Stopping at merely big when wumbo was within reach.

Go forth and wumbo.
