**wumbo.tech** is the worldwide leader in wumbology — the study of wumbo.

## wum·bo·lo·gy

_noun._ The study of wumbo.

> I wumbo, you wumbo, he/she/me wumbo.

Wumbology is an expansive discipline. To wumbo is to embiggen the ordinary into
the extraordinary; to take what is small and make it, well, wumbo. It is equal
parts imagination, confidence, and a willingness to point at a magnifying glass
and declare your intentions to the world.

2. **Wumborama** — a spectacular abundance of wumbo.
